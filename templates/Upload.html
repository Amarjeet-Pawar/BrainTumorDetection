About Brain Tumors

What is a Brain Tumor?

A brain tumor is a mass or growth of abnormal cells in your brain. They can be cancerous (malignant) or non-cancerous (benign). Early detection is crucial for effective treatment.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent headaches
  • Vision problems
  • Seizures
  • Balance difficulties
  • Speech problems

Prevention & Care

Early Detection Tips

  • Regular medical check-ups
  • MRI/CT scans for high-risk patients
  • Monitor persistent symptoms

Healthy Practices

  • Avoid unnecessary radiation exposure
  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Regular exercise
  • Manage stress levels
